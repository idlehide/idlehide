Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how IdleHide ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • License Information: Machine ID, name, and email address for license verification purposes
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, PayPal processes your payment information. We do not directly store your payment details
  • Website Usage Data: Through Google Analytics, we collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our website

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and verify software licenses
  • To process payments through PayPal
  • To analyze website traffic and improve our service
  • To communicate with you about your purchase or license
  • To provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website. Subject to GitHub's privacy policy
  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage patterns. You can opt-out through your browser settings
  • PayPal: Processes payments. Subject to PayPal's privacy policy

5. Software Data Collection

The IdleHide software itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or usage data from your computer beyond the initial license verification.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. License verification data is stored securely and is only used for its intended purpose.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and verify licenses. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics tracking

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our support channels listed on our website.
